History

Origin

Chess originated in India in the 6th century AD, and soon spread to Persia. When the Arabs conquered Persia, chess was taken up by the Muslim world and then spread to Southern Europe through the Muslims capturing of Spain.

History of Checkmate

At around the year 600 Chesss became know as chatrang in Persia, which then evolved to shatranj due to pronunciation problems.Then people started exclaiming Shāh (translates to "King") when the other oppents King was under attack (Known as Check now), and Shāh Māt (translates to "the King is helpless") when the Kings was under attack and could not escape (known as Checkmate today).
